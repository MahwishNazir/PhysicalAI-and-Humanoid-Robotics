# References

Further reading and resources for robotics and physical AI.

## Books

### Robotics Fundamentals

- Lynch, K. M., & Park, F. C. (2017). *Modern Robotics: Mechanics, Planning, and Control*. Cambridge University Press.
- Craig, J. J. (2017). *Introduction to Robotics: Mechanics and Control* (4th ed.). Pearson.
- Siciliano, B., Sciavicco, L., Villani, L., & Oriolo, G. (2010). *Robotics: Modelling, Planning and Control*. Springer.

### Humanoid Robotics

- Kajita, S., Hirukawa, H., Harada, K., & Yokoi, K. (2014). *Introduction to Humanoid Robotics*. Springer.
- Goswami, A., & Vadakkepat, P. (Eds.). (2019). *Humanoid Robotics: A Reference*. Springer.

### Physical AI and Embodied Intelligence

- Brooks, R. A. (1999). *Cambrian Intelligence: The Early History of the New AI*. MIT Press.
- Pfeifer, R., & Bongard, J. (2006). *How the Body Shapes the Way We Think: A New View of Intelligence*. MIT Press.

## Research Papers

### ROS and Robot Middleware

- Quigley, M., et al. (2009). "ROS: an open-source Robot Operating System." *ICRA Workshop on Open Source Software*.
- Macenski, S., et al. (2022). "Robot Operating System 2: Design, architecture, and uses in the wild." *Science Robotics*, 7(66).

### Humanoid Locomotion

- Kajita, S., et al. (2003). "Biped walking pattern generation by using preview control of zero-moment point." *IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation*.
- Vukobratović, M., & Borovac, B. (2004). "Zero-moment point—thirty five years of its life." *International Journal of Humanoid Robotics*, 1(1), 157-173.

### Sim-to-Real Transfer

- Tobin, J., et al. (2017). "Domain randomization for transferring deep neural networks from simulation to the real world." *IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems*.
- Peng, X. B., et al. (2018). "Sim-to-real: Learning agile locomotion for quadruped robots." *Robotics: Science and Systems*.

## Official Documentation

### Simulation Platforms

- [Gazebo Documentation](https://gazebosim.org/docs) - Gazebo robot simulator documentation

## Humanoid Robot Platforms

### Research Platforms

- [Boston Dynamics Atlas](https://bostondynamics.com/atlas/) - Advanced bipedal humanoid research platform
- [NASA Valkyrie](https://www.nasa.gov/feature/valkyrie) - NASA's humanoid robot for space exploration
- [Toyota T-HR3](https://global.toyota/en/newsroom/corporate/24930118.html) - Teleoperated humanoid robot

### Commercial Development

- [Tesla Optimus](https://www.tesla.com/AI) - General-purpose humanoid robot in development
- [Agility Robotics Digit](https://agilityrobotics.com/robots) - Bipedal robot for logistics

### Legacy Platforms

- [Honda ASIMO](https://global.honda/innovation/robotics/ASIMO.html) - Pioneer humanoid robot (retired 2022)
- [Boston Dynamics Petman](https://bostondynamics.com/petman/) - Anthropomorphic testing robot

## Tools and Libraries

### Python Robotics Libraries

- [Robotics Toolbox for Python](https://github.com/petercorke/robotics-toolbox-python) - Python tools for robot modeling and control
- [ikpy](https://github.com/Phylliade/ikpy) - Inverse kinematics library
- [NumPy](https://numpy.org/) - Numerical computing (essential for robotics calculations)

### Visualization

- [RViz2](https://github.com/ros2/rviz) - ROS 2 3D visualization tool
- [Matplotlib](https://matplotlib.org/) - Plotting library for data visualization
- [Plotly](https://plotly.com/python/) - Interactive plotting for robotics data
